Within Basta Fazoolin there are 4 menus. The brunch menu, early-bird menu, dinner menu, and kids menu. Each of these menus contain a dictionary of items that are available during their respecitve time frames. When you choose items 
from out of the selected menu you are then able to calculate the total bill for each item and it will be displayed in the terminal. 

Beyond the menus you are able to create franchies in certain locations and give them all 4 menus or just 1 menu to your liking. You are also abel to create businesses
which hold multiple franchies.
